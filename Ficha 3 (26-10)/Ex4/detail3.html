Positano is a stunning coastal town located on the Amalfi Coast in southern Italy. It is renowned for its breathtaking beauty, colorful cliffside buildings, and vibrant atmosphere. Here are some key points about Positano:

  • Scenic Beauty: Positano is celebrated for its picturesque setting on the steep cliffs of the Amalfi Coast. The town's pastel-colored buildings and narrow, winding streets make it a unique and beautiful destination. The crystal-clear waters of the Tyrrhenian Sea add to its charm.

  • Beaches: Positano boasts beautiful beaches, including Spiaggia Grande and Fornillo Beach. Tourists and locals alike enjoy sunbathing and swimming in the Mediterranean waters.

  • Cultural Sites: Positano has historical and cultural sites worth exploring, including the Church of Santa Maria Assunta. The church is famous for its 13th-century Byzantine icon of the Black Madonna.
