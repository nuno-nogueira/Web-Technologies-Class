Capri is a small, picturesque island located in the Tyrrhenian Sea, off the Sorrentine Peninsula on the southern coast of Italy. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, crystal-clear waters, and upscale lifestyle. Here are some key points about Capri:

  • Scenic Beauty: Capri is celebrated for its captivating landscapes, including rugged coastlines, limestone cliffs, and beautiful sea caves. The most famous of these is the Blue Grotto (Grotta Azzurra), a sea cave known for its brilliant blue waters.

  • Towns: The island has two main towns, Capri and Anacapri. The town of Capri is the main hub, known for its luxury shops, boutiques, and upscale hotels. Anacapri is a quieter, more relaxed town located at a higher elevation.

  • Shopping and Dining: Capri is a shopping destination, with many designer boutiques and local craft shops. The island also offers a variety of dining options, including seafood restaurants and local specialties like Caprese salad.
