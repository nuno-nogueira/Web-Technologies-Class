Ravello is a picturesque town located on the Amalfi Coast in southern Italy, in the Campania region. It is renowned for its stunning views, historical and cultural attractions, and beautiful gardens. Here are some key points about Ravello:

  • Scenic Beauty: Ravello is perched on a hilltop, providing breathtaking panoramic views of the Amalfi Coast and the Tyrrhenian Sea. The town's high vantage point offers some of the most spectacular vistas in Italy.

  • Ravello Festival: Ravello is famous for its annual Ravello Festival, which features a variety of musical and cultural events, including classical music concerts, art exhibitions, and other performances. The festival takes place from late spring to early autumn.

  • Cultural Sites: The town is home to several churches and historical sites, including the Ravello Cathedral (Duomo di Ravello) and the Church of Santa Maria a Gradillo.
